About Us

Neofolks

Neofolks is a joint initiative of young and passionate students at Navrachana University who came together to organize events with the motive of bringing out innovative ideas, developing technical skills and connecting like minded individuals together. The folks at neofolks along the way became the pillars for upholding the Hack Club NUV community.
Hack Club

Hack Club NUV is an inclusive community to support passionate techies just like you and I. This community aims at creating a collaborative learning environment where individuals can share knowledge. Hack Club NUV also organizes beginner-friendly tech events and hackathons locally at the college.
